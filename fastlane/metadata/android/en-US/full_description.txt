Haven is an open source SSH and SFTP client for Android.

Connect to your servers with a full terminal emulator, browse and transfer files over SFTP, and manage your SSH keys â€” all from your phone.

Features:
- SSH terminal with multi-session tab support
- SFTP file browser for uploading and downloading files
- SSH key generation and management (Ed25519, RSA, ECDSA)
- Biometric app lock (fingerprint or face)
- Configurable terminal font size
- Light, dark, and system-default themes
- Material You dynamic colors on Android 12+
- No ads, no tracking, no data collection

Haven connects only to servers you configure. All data stays on your device.
